Privacy Policy

At WorkBank, your privacy is very important to us. We collect only essential data such as your email for newsletters and job alerts. This information is never shared with third parties without your consent.

Job data displayed on our platform is aggregated from publicly available sources and not stored permanently. We use cookies only for core site functionality, such as keeping your preferences and improving user experience.

WorkBank complies with applicable data protection regulations, including the NDPR (Nigeria Data Protection Regulation). For any privacy concerns, please reach out to our support team via the contact page.
